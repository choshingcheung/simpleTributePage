Elon Musk

CEO of SpaceX & Tesla

Elon Musk, the wealthiest person in the world, with an estimated net worth of US$226 billion as of September 2023, according to the Bloomberg Billionaires Index, and $249 billion according to Forbes, primarily from his ownership stakes in both Tesla and SpaceX

Here is a time line of Elon Musk's life

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria
  • 1983 - Created a video game and sold it for 500$
  • 1989 - Started college in Ontario
  • 1994 - Got Wharton economics and physics degrees
  • 1999 - Starts X.com - an online bank with his own money
  • 2000 - Merges with his rival start-up Confinity to form PayPal
  • 2001 - Moved to LA to be closer to the space industry and learned how to build rockets from books
  • 2002 - After finding out that rockets are overpriced, the ambitious entrepreneur started Space X and got 250M from sale of PayPal
  • 2004 - Founded likeminded people and started his venture into electric cars - TESLA
  • 2006 - First rocket was launched and exploded. Moreover, he won a NASA contracts to demo delivery and became the first investor of Solar City - his cousin's company
  • 2007 - His second rocket was launched... and exploded again
  • 2008 - The first TESLA customers got their Roadsters at $109,000. What is more, another failed attempt at launching a rocket brought both TESLA and Space X to the crisis of running out of money. However, his fourth launch turned successful which won him a NASA contract for 12 flights for $1,6 Billion. In december 2008, hours before going bankrupt, Elon found funding for TESLA.
